Newsletters

The Nepean Blue Mountains U3A publishes a monthly Newsletter in which important information about our U3A is released. Items may include upcoming events, new courses offered, changes to existing courses and general interest items.

Members are encouraged to view/download the latest newsletter by selecting the appropriate year/month link below. The newsletter will appear in a separate window/tab.

Anyone wishing to submit a Newsletter item should forward the content to the Editor before the 22nd of the preceding month.
