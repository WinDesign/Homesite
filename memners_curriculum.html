Basic Computing at Jordan Springs Community Hub

We are able to accommodate a few more students at our Computer Class at Jordan Springs.

The Jordan Springs Community Hub offers wonderful facilities with free parking and a state-of-the-art computer room with laptops available for general use.

The course begins with the basics and progresses throughout the year. Subjects such as understanding hardware and software, use of the internet, filing documents/photos, e-mail, Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint, synchronising of computers with other devices and a general overview of technology.

This course was not covered in the Handbook this year but is in its fourth year and moved from Penrith to Jordan Springs last year.

Proposed New Course - Ageing Mindfully

This course is proposed by Val McCrae and would include discussion/ reflection and relaxation / meditation.

Having read widely on the topic of ageing from the perspectives of health, fitness, lifestyle, philosophy and spirituality - Val has the idea of presenting some examples of various viewpoints on these topics - and opening them to the group for discussion and reflection. This would be followed by a period of relaxation and meditation - based on a variety of strategies such as guided meditation, stress management and mindfulness -giving group members the experience of learning the techniques for the first time - or practicing in the company of others.

There are rooms available at Glenbrook Bowling club, Wednesdays 10:00am to 12:00noon. Interested members should contact Val.
