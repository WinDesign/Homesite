The Nepean-Blue Mountains U3A is a voluntary, not-for-profit organisation that offers older people low-cost educational, life-style, and recreational courses in a pleasant, supportive social setting. Our U3A is a self-help group, built on the premise that older people have the skills and knowledge to provide learning opportunities for themselves and others.

Interested, thinking of joining us, but need more information? Then browse through this site using the top menu or, use the quick link buttons below.

Welcome

Welcome

Welcome to the Nepean Blue Mountains University of the Third Age (U3A) web site. Here you will find details about joining us, or if already a member, updated information about events, details of new courses or changes to existing ones and links to our newsletter, forms and important documents.


For perspective new members don’t be put off by the name “University” because a U3A is not a university in the conventional sense. What it is, is a group of people pursuing shared interests under the guidance of tutor or activity leader where there are no prerequisites, no exams and no awards made. It’s all about participating, stimulating the “grey matter” or joining in activities in a pleasant social setting. Subjects can be academic, general interest, hobbies or physical activity. Above all else the idea is to enjoy your involvement in the U3A programs.


So, have a look around this site, there’s plenty of information to help you understand the workings of our U3A, how to join us and then select subjects or activities of your choice. The top menu has links to all relevant pages, is visible on all pages and is accessible from any position on any page. For direct links to important parts of this site you can use the Quick Link icons (images) below. There is also a Site Map which shows the overall structure of the site.
