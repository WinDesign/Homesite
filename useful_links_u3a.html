What is U3A

Useful links- U3A Networks

International and Australian U3A’s networks

WorldU3A – is an Internet service - not a membership organisation - and their online projects are available to all U3A groups or kindred organisations worldwide.

https://worldu3a.org/

U3A Alliance Australia is the national face of the U3A movement in Australia, bringing together the State and Territory networks of U3A groups throughout the country.

http://u3aaa.org/

U3A Australian Capital Territory (ACT) - The ACT has a single U3A. It includes members living in Queanbeyan and Yass and surrounds.

https://www.u3acanberra.org.au/

U3A Network NSW - The U3A Network NSW Inc. is an organisation of autonomous affiliated U3A groups in NSW and the ACT, and several more groups from interstate.

https://nsw.u3anet.org.au/

Queensland U3A Network - is the peak body representing member organisations across the State in metropolitan or regional locations.

http://www.u3aqld.org.au/home

U3A South Australia - is the peak body representing 19 U3A member organisations across the State in metropolitan or regional locations.

http://u3asouthaustralia.org.au/

U3A Network Victoria - is the peak body for 108 Member U3As representing around 38,000 members.

https://www.u3avictoria.com.au/

U3A Network WA - is the state-wide incorporated body covering all U3As in Western Australia.

https://u3anetworkwa.org.au/

U3A Online - is a virtual University of the Third Age delivering online learning via the Internet.

https://www.u3aonline.org.au/
