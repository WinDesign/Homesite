For members - Events

On this page you will find summary information about upcoming U3A events. The listing starts with items extending over the year, then recurring events, followed by events listed in chronologically order. Be aware that for some events, dates, venues and other details can change, so it's best you check the current Newsletter or contact us using the Contact Us page to verify details.


Will we will endeavour to keep the list current, but some displayed events may have already occurred and they will be removed on the next web site update.
