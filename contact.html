Course locations

Course Locations

Given the large geographic area our U3A serves and the number of courses offered, the venues where the classes and activities are conducted is dispersed; from St Mary’s to Blackheath. Location details for each course are given in our Courses Handbook and you can contact the tutor - activity leader or the U3A office for more details. Some courses are also held in private homes but for security reasons the addresses are not published. Again, contact the tutor or our office for further details.
